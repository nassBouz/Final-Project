Our Values

MISSION: MyEcole is the bay area's largest up and coming social network for Parents to connect with their kids school staff and monitor their kids evaluation. MyEcole also enables truly local conversations that empower Parents to build stronger and safer environnement for their kids.

VISION: Human beings are social by nature. We live in families and communities because it is in our nature. This basic need for connection is our guiding light what MyEcole are built on. We like to connect the whole parent's school to make every school stronger and safer.
